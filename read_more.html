Empowering IDP Camps: Enhancing Justice and Protection against SEA

Internally Displaced Persons face vulnerability to Sexual Exploitation and Abuse(SEA), and barriers to accessing justice. The Community Development and Accountability Center was a part of the HADAHANNU project has concluded training of community paralegals to improve access to justice for women and girls in IDP camps in the FCT.
